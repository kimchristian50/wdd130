About Us - Rio Grande White Water Rafting

Exciting rafting experiences in the Northern New Mexico area for people of all ages, including custom trips, delicious food, attention to detail, the highest of safety standards, and an exceptional guiding staff. We provide everything you need for an amazing outdoor recreational experience!

History

Rio Grande White Water Rafting Company was founded in 2006 by expert river guides Pam and Matt Christison. We have been exploring the waterways of the Southwest for the last 20 years, and we know them inside and out.

From the Rio Grande Racecourse to the Taox Box to the Rio Chama Canyon, we love to share the beauty of our waterways and would consider it an honor to work with your group on your own adventure.
