About Us - Rio Grande White Water Rafting

Exciting rafting experiences in the Northern New Mexico area for people of all ages, including custom trips, delicious food, attention to detail, the highest of safety standards, and an exceptional guiding staff. We provide everything you need for an amazing outdoor recreational experience!

History

Rio Grande White Water Rafting Company was founded in 2026 by expert river guides Pam and Matt Christison. We have been exploring the wild rivers of the Southwest for the last 20 years, and we know them inside and out. We love to share the beauty of our waterways and would consider it an honor to work with your group on your own adventure. Our trips include natural and cultural history of the region, combined with the thrills of rafting adventure. Our guiding staff are exceptional and will personalize your trip to make sure your group's experience exceeds your expectations.
