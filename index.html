New Mexico

"New Mexico is a southwestern U.S. state known for its striking desert and mountain landscapes, rich Native American and Hispanic heritage, and long, layered history. It is home to diverse geography ranging from high desert mesas and red rock formations to forested mountains, which contributes to a wide range of climates and outdoor activities. New Mexico culture reflects a blend of Indigenous, Spanish, Mexican, and Anglo influences, visible in its art, architecture, and cuisine. The state also plays an important role in science and national defense, with major research institutions and national laboratories, while maintaining a strong connection to tradition, land, and community." - wiki
