New Mexico

New Mexico is a southwestern U.S. state known for its striking desert and mountain landscapes, rich Native American and Hispanic heritage, and long, layered history. It is home to diverse geography ranging from high desert mesas and red rock formations to forested mountains. The state also plays an important role in science and national defense, with major research institutions and national laboratories, while maintaining a strong connection to tradition, land, and community.
